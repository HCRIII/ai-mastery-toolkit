## Overview

This folder contains documented multi-prompt workflows that show how to chain together prompts from the library for complex, real-world tasks. Each workflow provides step-by-step guidance, expected time investments, and success indicators.

## Available Workflows

### 01-research-deep-dive-workflow
**Purpose:** Conduct comprehensive research from initial exploration through synthesis and knowledge integration.

**Best for:**
- Technical or business topic research
- Strategic decision-making preparation
- Building domain expertise
- Creating research-based content

**Time:** 4-30 hours depending on depth
**Key Outcome:** Permanent notes integrated into knowledge system, actionable recommendations
**Slash Command:** `/research-deep-dive`

---

### 02-content-creation-pipeline-workflow
**Purpose:** Transform research and expertise into high-quality published content with systematic quality control.

**Best for:**
- Thought leadership articles
- Technical blog posts
- Educational materials
- Newsletter content

**Time:** 3-20 hours depending on complexity
**Key Outcome:** Publication-ready content with meta content for distribution
**Slash Command:** `/content-creation-pipeline`

---

### 03-client-project-lifecycle-workflow
**Purpose:** Manage client projects from discovery through delivery with clear communication and scope control.

**Best for:**
- Consulting engagements
- Custom development projects
- Professional services delivery
- Retainer client management

**Time:** 15-20% project overhead for communication
**Key Outcome:** Successful delivery, happy client, captured learnings
**Slash Command:** `/client-project-lifecycle`

---

### 05-iterative-document-approval
**Purpose:** Structured workflow for iterative document refinement and approval.

**Best for:**
- Complex documents requiring multiple rounds
- Proposals and reports needing stakeholder feedback
- Documents with specific formatting requirements
- Collaborative writing projects

**Time:** Varies by document complexity
**Key Outcome:** Polished, approved document meeting all requirements
**Slash Command:** `/iterative-document-approval`

---

### concurrent-asset-generation
**Purpose:** Generate core assets + all outbound distribution materials simultaneously while context is hot.

**Best for:**
- Product or course launches
- Multi-channel marketing campaigns
- Content releases requiring consistent messaging
- Any situation with 6+ distribution channels

**Time:** 4-6 hours total (vs 15+ hours sequential)
**Key Outcome:** Complete launch package with perfect consistency across all channels
**Effectiveness:** 98% (70-80% time savings, 70-80% token savings)

---

### weekly-review
**Purpose:** Comprehensive weekly review to maintain clarity, update systems, and plan effectively.

**Best for:**
- GTD practitioners
- Knowledge workers managing multiple projects
- Anyone feeling overwhelmed or unclear on priorities
- Maintaining work-life integration

**Time:** 60-90 min (quick) to 2-3 hours (standard) to 4-5 hours (quarterly)
**Key Outcome:** Clarity on priorities, trusted systems, learning captured
**Slash Command:** `/weekly-review`

## How to Use These Workflows

### First Time
1. Read the entire workflow to understand the full process
2. Identify which steps apply to your specific situation
3. Gather required inputs before starting
4. Block appropriate time (don't rush complex workflows)
5. Follow steps sequentially the first time

### After Familiarity
- Skip steps that don't apply to your current needs
- Adapt based on your context (workflows are templates, not rigid rules)
- Create your own variations for repeated use cases
- Time block regular workflows (e.g., Weekly Review every Friday)

## Workflow Selection Guide

**"I need to understand a topic deeply..."**
→ Use Research Deep Dive Workflow

**"I want to create an article/post/presentation..."**
→ Use Content Creation Pipeline Workflow

**"I'm starting/managing a client project..."**
→ Use Client Project Lifecycle Workflow

**"I need to get organized and clarify priorities..."**
→ Use weekly-review

**"I'm iterating on a document with feedback..."**
→ Use 05-iterative-document-approval

**"I'm launching a product/course/campaign..."**
→ Use concurrent-asset-generation

**"I'm doing a combination of these..."**
→ Workflows can be chained! For example:
- Research → Content (learn, then teach)
- Weekly Review → Client Lifecycle (plan, then execute)
- Client Lifecycle → Content (case study from project)
- Iterative Document → Client Lifecycle (refine, then deliver)
- Content Creation → Concurrent Asset Generation (create core, then distribute everywhere)

## Creating Your Own Workflows

To create a custom workflow:

1. **Identify the outcome** - What's the goal?
2. **Break into phases** - What are the major stages?
3. **Select prompts** - Which prompts accomplish each step?
4. **Define inputs/outputs** - What goes in and out of each step?
5. **Estimate time** - How long does each phase take?
6. **Identify success indicators** - How do you know it worked?
7. **Document tips** - What do you wish you'd known?
