## Step 3: Confirmation Interview

After presenting the overview, interview the user. Ask questions ONE AT A TIME, wait for response, follow up as needed.

**Questions to prepare (8-12, tailored to what the data shows):**

**Learning (2-3):**
- Confirm key learnings are accurate
- "What surprised you most this week?"
- Probe for insights not captured in the data

**Priorities (2-3):**
- Validate priority shifts observed
- "Looking at next week, what needs to move up or down?"
- Check alignment between stated and actual priorities

**Progress (2-3):**
- "What are you most proud of this week?"
- Identify what "counts" as progress to user
- Confirm major accomplishments

**Behavioral Development (2-3):**
- "Did you notice moments where the collaboration felt qualitatively different?"
- "Are there behavioral patterns you're seeing that the tracking system isn't capturing yet?"
- Confirm observed behavioral shifts match user's experience

**Open-Ended (1):**
- "What else should I know about this week that the data doesn't capture?"

**Interview Protocol:**
- Ask questions ONE AT A TIME
- Wait for user response after each
- Don't rush — let user think
- Take notes on responses for final synthesis
- Ask clarifying follow-ups as needed
