## Purpose
Analyze and evaluate content, systems, code, research, and ideas to extract insights, identify patterns, and make informed assessments.

## Categories

### Business/
Analyze business processes, sales calls, product feedback, candidate evaluations
- **When to use:** Evaluating business performance, interviewing, product development

### Content/
Analyze writing quality, debates, presentations, comments, discussions
- **When to use:** Evaluating prose, speeches, debates, audience reactions

### Research/
Analyze academic papers, claims, arguments, legislation, propositions
- **When to use:** Research evaluation, truth-testing, policy analysis

### Security/
Analyze malware, security incidents, threat reports, email headers, risk
- **When to use:** Cybersecurity analysis, threat intelligence, risk assessment

### Technical/
Analyze logs, infrastructure code, technical submissions, systems
- **When to use:** Technical debugging, system evaluation, architecture review

## Common Use Cases
- Evaluate research paper quality and methodology
- Analyze writing for style and clarity
- Assess security threats and incidents
- Review technical logs and infrastructure
- Evaluate business metrics and feedback
