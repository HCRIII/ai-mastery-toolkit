## Purpose
Academic research, paper analysis, systematic thinking frameworks, and scholarly work.

## Categories

### Paper_Analysis/
Analyze academic papers (see 01_Analysis/Research)
- Analyze papers with rigor evaluation and quality assessment

### Research_Tools/
Research methodologies and systematic thinking
- **capture_thinkers_work** - Analyze philosophers and their teachings
- **answer_interview_question** - Generate expert interview responses
- **identify_dsrp_*** - DSRP (Distinctions, Systems, Relationships, Perspectives) analysis

### Academic_Writing/
Create academic content (see 02_Creation/Content)
- Create academic papers in LaTeX format

## Note
Academic prompts are distributed across functional categories:
- **Paper analysis** → 01_Analysis/Research/
- **Paper summarization** → 04_Summarization/Research/
- **Academic writing** → 02_Creation/Content/

This category contains specialized research tools and cross-references.

## Common Use Cases
- Analyze research paper quality
- Apply systematic thinking frameworks (DSRP)
- Capture philosophical teachings
- Generate expert-level responses
- Create academic papers
