## Purpose
Self-assessment, personal growth, reflection tools, and wellness-oriented prompts (t_* prefix indicates therapy/thinking focus).

## Categories

### Self_Assessment/
Identify blindspots, negative thinking, Dunning-Kruger effects, metrics
- **t_check_dunning_kruger** - Assess confidence vs competence
- **t_find_blindspots** - Identify thinking blindspots
- **t_find_negative_thinking** - Detect negative thought patterns
- **t_check_metrics** - Evaluate personal metrics
- **t_analyze_challenge_handling** - Analyze how you handle challenges

### Growth_Planning/
Career planning, goal setting, mission visualization
- **t_create_h3_career** - Create career strategy (Horizons 1, 2, 3)
- **t_find_neglected_goals** - Identify overlooked objectives
- **t_visualize_mission_goals_projects** - Map personal mission to projects

### Reflection_Tools/
Year reviews, life outlook, encouragement, threat modeling
- **t_year_in_review** - Annual reflection and assessment
- **t_describe_life_outlook** - Articulate life philosophy
- **t_give_encouragement** - Generate personalized encouragement
- **t_red_team_thinking** - Challenge your own plans
- **t_threat_model_plans** - Identify risks in personal plans
- **heal_person** - Wellness and healing perspective

## When to Use
- Annual or quarterly self-reflection
- Career planning and strategy
- Identifying personal growth areas
- Challenging your own assumptions
- Seeking perspective on life direction

## Common Use Cases
- Year-end reviews and planning
- Career transitions and planning
- Identify cognitive biases
- Personal goal alignment
- Life direction clarity
