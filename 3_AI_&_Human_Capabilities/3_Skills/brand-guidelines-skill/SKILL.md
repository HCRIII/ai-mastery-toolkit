# Brand Guidelines Skill - Anthropic Branding

## What This Skill Does

Applies Anthropic's official brand colors and typography to artifacts that benefit from Anthropic's look-and-feel, ensuring brand consistency and visual formatting according to company design standards.

## When to Use This Skill

**Automatic triggers:**
- "Anthropic brand", "company design standards"
- Brand colors or style guidelines mentions

**Use when you need:**
- Anthropic-branded materials
- Company design standards
- Official look-and-feel
- Branded presentations
- Corporate materials

## How to Invoke

**Explicit invocation:**
```
Use the brand-guidelines skill
```

**Trigger words (automatic):**
- "Apply Anthropic branding..."
- "Use company brand guidelines..."
- "Make this look like Anthropic materials..."

## Capabilities

- **Brand Colors:** Official Anthropic color palette
- **Typography:** Approved font selections
- **Design Standards:** Company visual guidelines
- **Consistency:** Unified brand appearance
- **Professional:** Corporate-quality output
