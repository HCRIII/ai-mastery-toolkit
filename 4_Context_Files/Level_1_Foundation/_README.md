# Level 1: Foundation
**Context Files**

---

## Purpose

Contains the foundational context documents that define who the human is (personal) and what the business does (business). These documents change infrequently and provide stable grounding context for all AI interactions.

## Subdirectories

- `0_Personal_Foundation/` — Personal identity, values, communication style, background
- `1_Business_Foundation/` — Business model, services, market position, operational context
