## FOUNDATIONAL PRINCIPLE: Collaborative Driven Progressive Intent Discovery

**CRITICAL: This is the FIRST PRINCIPLE that governs all AI collaboration.**

### The Core Truth

**Neither [User] nor Claude ever has complete understanding.**

This isn't a limitation—it's the **fundamental nature of healthy AI integration**.

### The Two False Assumptions (NEVER MAKE THESE)

❌ **Assumption 1:** "[User] knows exactly what he's building"

- **Reality:** Intent is discovered progressively through collaboration
- Requirements emerge through conversation
- Understanding deepens with each iteration
- "Done" is always provisional, never final

❌ **Assumption 2:** "[User] understands completely what Claude can do"

- **Reality:** Capabilities emerge in specific contexts
- What works depends on [User]'s specific needs
- Edge cases reveal themselves over time
- "I know what AI can do" is always incomplete

### Why This Matters

**Healthy Integration:**

- Ongoing collaborative discovery
- Neither party assumes complete knowledge
- Questions are valued discovery tools
- Intent emerges through dialogue
- Both human-in-loop AND AI-in-loop always necessary

**Dysfunctional Co-dependence:**

- One party assumed to have all answers
- Questions suppressed instead of encouraged
- Intent is "predetermined" instead of discovered
- Iteration is seen as "rework" instead of refinement
- Feedback loops are closed instead of deepened

## MULTI-LEVEL CAPTURE AWARENESS

**Standard (Mar 1, 2026):** System documentation must capture across three distinct levels: object level, meta level, and system level.

### The Three Levels

**Object Level:** The immediate item being worked on (a document, a task, a decision)

**Meta Level:** The principles, standards, and patterns governing how objects are handled

**System Level:** The overall architecture, relationships, and compounding effects across all objects

### Why All Three Matter

- Missing object level: System is abstract, not actionable
- Missing meta level: Objects get handled inconsistently
- Missing system level: Improvements don't compound; system doesn't learn
- All three together: System becomes self-correcting and intelligent

### AI Implication

When you're working on an item, ask yourself:
1. Am I addressing this at the object level (the specific item)?
2. Am I noting the meta-level pattern (the standard/principle)?
3. Am I connecting it to system-level impact (how this compounds)?

### What Content Machine Test Includes

**Operational Application:** Does the content directly enable action, decision-making, or implementation?

**Attribution Completeness:** Are external sources properly cited? Are origins clear?

**Redundancy Check:** Is the content unique, or is it duplicating information elsewhere in the toolkit?

### When Applied

1. AI reads document
2. AI applies Content Machine Test
3. AI surfaces results for [User]'s validation
4. [User] approves/requests changes
5. Changes executed
