## 1. Offering Context Summary

**Transformation promise:** Resolve a specific, bounded transformation challenge through the full Cognitive Architecture methodology in 12 weeks; bi-weekly sessions plus async support.
**Primary audience segment:** ICP-Coaching — high-performing entrepreneurs with a specific challenge; $5,000–7,500 investment capacity; prior content exposure preferred.
**Primary journey stages served:** Stage 3 (Decision — discovery call) through Stage 6 (Completion/Transition)
**Content goal:** Drive qualified discovery call bookings from the warm audience (community members, course alumni, email subscribers) and referral network. Not a mass-funnel offer — content is targeted and relational, not broadcast.

**Best practices basis:** High-ticket coaching ($5K–$25K) converts primarily through trust-accumulated content (12–20 week pre-existing relationship), social proof (case studies, transformation stories), and direct outreach. Webinars convert 15–20% of attendees to discovery calls. Broadcast marketing alone does not close at this price point — personal outreach and relationship sequences are essential conversion drivers.

## 2. Channel Content Plan

### LinkedIn (Channel 07)
**Guideline reference:** `Level_5_Content_Strategy/07_LinkedIn/LinkedIn_Guidelines_v1.md`
**Content role for this offering:** Consideration → Decision (warm audience trust-building; not direct sales)
**Content types:**
- Transformation Story Posts — 1–2x/month; client outcome framing without naming client; "A client came to me with X. 12 weeks later, Y." Pattern: Problem → Methodology → Outcome → Reframe
- Methodology Explainer Posts — 1x/week; explain one aspect of how the Intensive works or what it addresses; soft discovery call CTA in first comment only
- Social Proof / Results Thread — 1x/month; 5-post thread walking through a transformation arc

**Cadence:** 2–3 posts/week on LinkedIn (this offering contributes 2–4 posts/month; remainder comes from authority and community content)
**CTA pattern:** No CTA in post body. First comment only: "If you're navigating something like this, a 15-min discovery conversation might be the right next step. Link in my profile."
**Volume (per quarter):** 4–6 transformation stories; 8–12 methodology explainers; 2–3 results threads

### Email List (Channel 04)
**Guideline reference:** `Level_5_Content_Strategy/04_Email-List/Email-List_Guidelines_v1.md`
**Content role for this offering:** Decision (hot segment nurture → discovery call)
**Content types:**
- Coaching Prospect Nurture Sequence — 3 emails over 3 weeks (triggered when subscriber signals high intent: replies to multiple emails, clicks discovery call links, asks coaching questions)
  - Email 1 (Day 0): "What the Intensive addresses" — problem/transformation framing; no pitch
  - Email 2 (Day 7): "How it works" — methodology overview, what 12 weeks looks like, what clients say
  - Email 3 (Day 21): "Is this right for you?" — honest qualification criteria; discovery call CTA
- Case Study Email — 1–2x/quarter to full list; transformation story; understated soft CTA for discovery call
- Application Announcement — 1x/quarter to full list when accepting new clients; direct but low-pressure

**Cadence:** Prospect nurture is triggered (not broadcast). Case study and application emails: 4–8x/year total.
**CTA pattern:** Inline text link: "If this resonates, book a 15-min discovery conversation here." No countdown timers, no urgency/scarcity language.
**Volume (per quarter):** 1 prospect nurture sequence (3 emails); 1–2 case study emails; 1 application announcement

### Personal Network Outreach (Channel 17)
**Guideline reference:** `Level_5_Content_Strategy/17_Personal-Network-Outreach/Personal-Network-Outreach_Guidelines_v1.md`
**Content role for this offering:** Decision (highest-conversion channel at this price point)
**Content types:**
- Network Reactivation — 4–8/month; reconnect with past contacts who may be ready for this work; reference shared history; no pitch in first email
- Warm Introduction Requests — 1–2/month; ask existing clients or contacts to introduce qualified prospects; write the introduction email for them
- Direct Coaching Inquiry — 1–2/month; to clearly qualified contacts who have signaled readiness; brief, specific, no pressure

**Cadence:** 4–8 personalized outreach emails/week (mixed types); max 2 follow-ups per contact
**CTA pattern:** "Would love to catch up. Free for 20 minutes in the next few weeks?" or "Curious if this is relevant to what you're working on. No pressure — just wanted to share."
**Volume (per month):** 16–32 outreach emails; 4–8 introduction requests; 4–8 direct coaching inquiries

## 4. Launch Phase vs. Evergreen Plan

**This offering is always-on (not launch-based).** New client capacity is accepted quarterly.

**Quarterly cycle:**
- Weeks 1–8: Authority + methodology content builds warm audience on LinkedIn; email list receives case study and synthesis content
- Week 8–10: Application announcement email sent; direct outreach to qualified contacts initiated
- Weeks 10–12: Discovery call conversations; intake for next cohort/quarter
- Ongoing: Network reactivation and introduction requests run continuously at 4–8/week

**Upward feedback triggers:** When discovery call patterns reveal recurring objections or readiness signals not yet addressed in content → flag to `## Future Inputs` in LinkedIn_Guidelines_v1.md and Email-List_Guidelines_v1.md.
