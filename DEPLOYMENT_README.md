## Setup Checklist (Complete in Order)

### Step 1 — Read the toolkit foundation
- [ ] Read `0_Start_Here/` — understand the system before modifying anything
- [ ] Read `1_Toolkit_Documentation/` — understand documentation standards
- [ ] Skim `2_How_To_Guides/` — know what guidance is available

### Step 2 — Fill in your context
- [ ] Complete `4_Context_Files/Personal_Preferences_v1.md` with your preferences
- [ ] Complete `4_Context_Files/SuperContext_v1.md` Layer 1 (Identity) and Layer 2 (Active Projects)
- [ ] Note: Layers 3–7 of SuperContext will fill in naturally as you work

### Step 3 — Run your first session
- [ ] Open Claude (or your AI of choice)
- [ ] Load your Personal Preferences and SuperContext
- [ ] Run the `session-startup` prompt from `3_AI_&_Human_Capabilities/1_Prompts/00-core-system/session-startup/`
- [ ] Confirm the echo output reflects your context correctly

### Step 4 — Begin building your Context Files
- [ ] Review `4_Context_Files/Level_0_Map_&_Preferences/` to understand the Context Files structure
- [ ] Start with Level 1 Foundation documents (your identity, mission, and business foundations)
- [ ] Add entries to `_AI_Context_Index.md` as you create documents

### Step 5 — Establish your System Wisdom baseline
- [ ] After your first 3–5 sessions, assess your behavioral baselines in `5_System_Wisdom/4_System_Intelligence/`
- [ ] Review the behavioral taxonomy frameworks in `5_System_Wisdom/1_Human_Behavioral_Taxonomy/`, `2_AI_Behavioral_Taxonomy/`, and `3_Dyadic_Dynamic_Taxonomy/`
- [ ] Set up weekly review cadence (recommended: Sundays)
