## Research

**CDPID — Collaborative Driven Progressive Intent Discovery**
A behavioral framework for AI-integrated professional development. Describes the progressive, collaborative discovery of intent as the operating mechanism of effective human-AI collaboration.
- DOI: [10.2139/ssrn.6278438](https://ssrn.com/abstract=6278438)
- Platforms: OSF · SSRN · ResearchGate · Academia.edu · Zenodo

**Longitudinal Human-AI Behavioral Development Research**
26+ months of versioned behavioral tracking across three interdependent systems: practitioner psychology, AI calibration state, and human-AI dyadic relationship patterns. The dataset distinguishes *operational* AI fluency (effective use today) from *developmental* AI fluency (measurably improving the human-AI system over time) — a category absent from existing AI fluency frameworks. Data is embedded in the AI Mastery Toolkit repository.
