**This document is designed to be empty.** It is a template for clients to fill out with their own information, creating a personalized SuperContext that makes their AI collaboration more effective.

**Purpose:** This template helps clients document their professional context, values, working preferences, strategic goals, and active projects so that AI can provide more relevant and personalized assistance.

**How to Use:**
- Save a copy of this template for your personal use
- Fill in the sections that are most relevant to your work
- Start with the basics (SC_Layer 1-3) and build over time
- Update regularly as your context evolves
- Reference this document when starting new AI collaboration sessions

**Note for Coaches/Consultants:** Provide this blank template to clients as part of their AI mastery toolkit. Guide them through completing the most critical sections first, then encourage iterative refinement.
