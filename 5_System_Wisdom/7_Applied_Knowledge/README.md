# Applied Knowledge

**Purpose:** Assimilated intelligence transformed into learning materials, teaching frameworks, and structured understanding. This is where raw System Intelligence gets synthesized into higher-level knowledge that can be taught, shared, and published.

**The Knowledge Progression:**

```
Taxonomy Snapshots (raw data)
    ↓ pattern extraction
System Intelligence (validated patterns, principles, anti-patterns)
    ↓ synthesis and structuring
Applied Knowledge (teaching materials, frameworks, publishable insights)
    ↓ narrative construction
Publications (white papers, articles, book chapters)
```

**What Lives Here:**

- **Teaching Frameworks** — CDPID concepts translated into learnable, teachable structures. How would you explain Collaborative Load Equilibration to a client? What exercises demonstrate Prompt Aptness development? These are the pedagogical translations of the research.

- **Practice Guides** — Step-by-step guidance for developing specific capabilities. Not the theoretical taxonomy (that lives in the Comprehensive Taxonomy files) but the practical "how to get from Level 2 to Level 3" material.

- **Synthesis Documents** — Periodic synthesis of accumulated System Intelligence into coherent narratives. Weekly or monthly reflections that weave individual patterns into larger developmental stories.

- **Book Material** — Over time, this folder becomes the raw material for writing the book based on the research. Chapter drafts, structural outlines, key arguments, and supporting evidence drawn from the entire System Wisdom corpus.

- **Client-Ready Material** — Frameworks, assessments, and guides that can be given to clients to implement CDPID principles in their own human-AI collaboration practice.

**Relationship to Other Folders:**

- Draws FROM: 4_System_Intelligence (validated patterns), 5_Case_Evidence (illustrative examples), 1-3 Taxonomy folders (developmental frameworks)
- Feeds INTO: 4_Context_Files Level 4-6 (offering specs, content strategy, asset production) and external publications

**What Does NOT Live Here:**

- Raw data or snapshots (those stay in the taxonomy folders)
- Unvalidated patterns (those stay in System Intelligence until validated)
- Operational documents (those live in 4_Context_Files)

**Folder Contents:**

- `Teaching_Frameworks/` — Pedagogical translations of CDPID concepts
- `Practice_Guides/` — Capability development guides by dimension
- `Synthesis/` — Periodic narrative synthesis of accumulated intelligence
- `Book/` — Chapter drafts, outlines, and supporting material
- `Client_Materials/` — Ready-to-share frameworks and assessments
- `_Archive/` — Superseded materials

**Design Note:** This folder will be sparse initially and grow as the system accumulates enough intelligence to synthesize. That's by design — you can't teach what you haven't learned, and you can't synthesize what you haven't observed. The Applied Knowledge folder is the compound return on the investment the other folders represent.
