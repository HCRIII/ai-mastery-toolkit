## How to Use This Registry

**When adding a new source:**
- Confirm the source has demonstrated sustained quality (not just one good episode)
- Assign the next sequential EIS-SRC-00X ID
- Complete all fields: type, domain relevance, host/author, why it earns standing, cross-references, access
- Add to Registry Log

**When researching a topic:**
- Check this registry before assuming no relevant source is tracked
- If a registered source has covered a topic directly, note the specific episode or resource if known
- Cross-reference against Research Library for any entries sourced from registered sources

**What earns entry:**
- Consistent quality signal in [Your Organization]'s relevant domains (longevity, AI, performance, psychology, exponential tech, business strategy)
- Host/author has demonstrated domain credibility (research, track record, or framework utility)
- [User] actively attends to the source — not theoretical or archived

**What does NOT earn entry:**
- One-off excellent content with no recurring signal
- General interest sources without [Your Organization] domain relevance
- Sources [User] has sampled but does not monitor
