## How This File Works

One entry per published piece once results are available. Results are captured via the `outreach-content` system prompt Step 4 — either at the time of publication or deferred to the next session.

**What this file feeds:**
- Rotation calendar adjustments (which IP-segment combinations produce highest-converting outcomes)
- Channel concentration decisions (which channels produce the most email signups)
- Hook optimization (which angles create strongest recognition response)
- ICP calibration (which segments are most engaged across which concepts)

**Update protocol:** Log results within 48 hours of publication. Even zero-result entries are valuable — they establish a baseline and confirm the channel is active.

## Results Entry Template

Copy and complete for each published piece with available results:

```
## [YYYY-MM-DD] — [Channel] — [IP Concept]

**Asset File:** [Level 6 file path]
**Published URL/Location:** [URL, post link, or platform reference]
**Days Since Published:** [N]

**Quantitative Results:**
- Views / Impressions: [N]
- Engagements (likes + comments + shares/reposts): [N]
- Link clicks (to landing page): [N]
- Email signups attributed (within 48h of post): [N]
- Direct responses (DMs, comments requesting more): [N]

**Qualitative Observations:**
- [What comments or responses indicated about which pain point landed most viscerally]
- [Any specific language from responses that should be reflected back in future content]
- [Any unexpected segment engagement — people who aren't the primary target responding]

**Signal Extracted:**
- ICP resonance: [Which segment appears most engaged?]
- Pain point: [Which named pain produced the strongest recognition response?]
- IP angle: [Which framing of the IP concept resonated most?]
- Channel efficiency: [Did this channel produce disproportionate or underwhelming results?]

**Rotation Calendar Implication:**
- [Should this IP concept be prioritized in next rotation cycle? Why?]
- [Should segment targeting be adjusted for next deployment of this concept?]
- [Any hook or angle adjustment indicated by the signal?]
```
